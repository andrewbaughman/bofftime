Explanation

Uptick shows you the current time of day. Time in Uptick looks different because it's not counting up to 12 or 24 hours. It's counting up to 100,000 ticks.

Uptick uses Uniform Place-value Time, so it represents the time of day as a simple place-value number, a number of ticks.

By default, the status box shows there are 100,000 ticks in a day and each tick lasts 0.864 seconds.

You can tap the status box to change the place-value system and tap it again to hide the controls. Hide this explanation by swiping down.
